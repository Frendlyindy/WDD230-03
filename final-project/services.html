Service Missionaries

Service missionaires play a vital role in the church. Some work as temple workers or just generally serve near temples. We offer a special living package to service missionaries that want to stay with us.

  • Everything Offered in a Twin Room
  • Mini Kitchen with Fridge, stove, oven and microwave.
  • Laundry Services
  • Temple Shuttle Service Priority
  • Meal plan for those not wanting to cook often.
